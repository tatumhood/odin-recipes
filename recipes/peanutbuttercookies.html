3-Ingredient Peanut Butter Cookies

Description

The peanut butter cookies draw their name from their main ingredient: peanut butter. The cookies are light brown in colour and have a “cross” shape on top, formed when the dough is pierced with a fork. Today, we'll be talking about the origin of the peanut butter cookies, their usage and their history.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup peaunt butter
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the peanut butter, sugar, and egg.
  3. Scoop out of a spoonful of dough and roll it into a ball. Place the cookie balls onto a nonstick baking sheet.
  4. Flatten the cookies by pressing a fork down on the cookie to make a criss-cross pattern.
  5. Remove from baking sheet and cool.
  6. Enjoy!
